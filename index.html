WPI Running Club

We are a club that promotes and participates in recreational running at WPI. Everyone regardless of running ability is welcomed.

Our club was founded in the Spring of 2013. The purpose of the Running Club is to bring together WPI students who are interested in running and provide an environment which supports personal growth and camaraderie. We create a social and constructive atmosphere with regular practices and meetings. Today, we have over 60 members.

Ask to join our mailing list by emailing running-execs@wpi.edu
